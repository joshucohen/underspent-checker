Underspent Fund Checker

Identify funds that may not be spending at a sustainable rate

What This Tool Does

This tool helps identify funds that may be underutilized over time by analyzing multi-year balance and spending data. It calculates average balances, average spending, and a spend rate to highlight funds that may require review or action.

It is designed to support better financial stewardship and help institutions identify areas where funds may be accumulating faster than they are being used.

Who This Is For

This tool is designed for professionals working in higher education and nonprofit environments, including:

  • Advancement and Foundation teams
  • Finance and budget offices
  • Department administrators and fund managers
  • Anyone responsible for monitoring fund utilization

No technical experience is required. The tool is built to be simple, intuitive, and accessible.

What Type of Funds This Analyzes

This tool is best suited for analyzing philanthropic or institutional funds that:

  • Maintain a recurring balance over time
  • Support ongoing programs, scholarships, or operations
  • Are expected to be used in alignment with donor intent or institutional priorities

It is particularly useful for identifying funds that may be accumulating balances without corresponding spending activity.

Why This Was Built

This tool was built by a higher education professional who works directly with philanthropic funds and financial operations on a daily basis.

Many institutions face a common challenge: understanding whether funds are being used effectively and in alignment with their intended purpose. This often requires manual, time-consuming analysis across multiple systems and datasets.

This tool was created to simplify that process and make it accessible to anyone, without requiring technical expertise or complex data workflows.

How to Use

  1. Download the template using the button below
  2. Export your data from your system of record (CRM, ERP, or finance system)
    • You need fund-level data by year
    • Each year should include: fund_id, balance, spend, revenue
  3. Paste your data into the template
    • Do not rename sheets
    • Do not change column names
    • Fill all three sheets (Year1, Year2, Year3)
  4. Upload the completed file
  5. Click Analyze to generate results

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I get this data?
Export fund-level financial data from your system of record (such as your CRM, ERP, or financial system). You will need balances and spending data by year.

What is “Spend Rate”?
Spend Rate is calculated as Average Spend divided by Available Balance.

What does “underspent” mean?
Funds with low spend rates may indicate underutilization and may require review.

What if my file doesn’t work?
Ensure you are using the provided template exactly as structured.

Data Privacy

Your data is not stored, logged, or shared.

All uploaded files are processed temporarily in memory and discarded immediately after analysis. No data is accessible to developers or any third parties.
