Underspent Fund Checker

Quickly identify underutilized funds across multiple years

How it works

Upload a dataset with 3 years per fund (one row per year). The tool calculates average spending relative to available balance and flags funds below your selected threshold.

Analyze Your Data









Template format: fund_id, year, balance, spend, revenue

Definitions

  • Threshold (%): Maximum % of funds spent before flagged as underspent
  • Include Revenue: Adds incoming funds to available balance
  • Underspent: Spending rate below selected threshold
  • Requirements: Exactly 3 years per fund, positive balances only
